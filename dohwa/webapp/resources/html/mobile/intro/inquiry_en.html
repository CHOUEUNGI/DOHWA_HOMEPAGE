Guide to Personal Information Collection
and Use

1. Personal Information Handling Guidelines

01. General Provisions

“Personal information” refers to the personally identifiable information such as name, resident registration number, and videos (including videos that cannot be used to identify a person by themselves but can easily be combined with other information to identify that person). “Data subject” refers to the person who may be identified by the handled data and serves as the agent of the same data. The company ensures that the Personal Information Handling Guidelines remains easily identifiable any time by disclosing them on the main page of its website (http:///www.dohwa.co.kr). If it revises the Personal Information Handling Guidelines, the company will announce it through Notices on its website (or through announcements to individual users).

02. Personal Information Items Processed and Their Purpose

The company does not process sensitive information that may seriously infringe on privacy or personally identifiable data that can identify an individual in accordance with the relevant law or regulation or without the consent of the data subject.

A. Items Processed
Name (Korean/Sino-Korean/English), photo, password, home phone number, home address, permanent domicile, mobile phone number, email address, education, military service (completed or not, service period, service category, rank), language skills, computer skills, qualifications, family relations, eligibility for veteran’s benefits, work experience, social relations, honors, overseas experience, research experience

B. Purpose of Data Processing
- For recruiting procedures or to communicate with applicants
- Human resources management including payment of salary, provision of welfare benefits, training, subscription to credit guarantee insurance, support for implementation and evaluation of various tasks

03. Personal Information Processing and Retention Period

By principle, when the purpose of the collection and use of the personal information is fulfilled, the specific data shall be destroyed immediately. However, the data below shall be retained for respectively specified periods, and when necessary, the consent of the data subject shall be obtained.

A. Personal information of a Hired Job Applicant
- Retention period: Permanently
- Reason for retention: Human resources management during the person’s employment, welfare benefits after retirement, issuance of employment certificate, etc.
- Items retained: Name (Korean/Sino-Korean/English), photo, password, home phone number, home address, permanent domicile, mobile phone number, email address, education, military service (completed or not, service period, service category, rank), language skills, computer skills, qualifications, family relations, eligibility for veteran’s benefits, work experience, social relations, honors, overseas experience, research experience

B. Personal Information of a Job Applicant Who Is not Hired
- Retention period: 5 years from the completion of the recruiting procedure
- Reasons for retention: For management of human resources that may be hired in the future
- Name (Korean/Sino-Korean/English), photo, password, home phone number, home address, permanent domicile, mobile phone number, email address, education, military service (completed or not, service period, service category, rank), language skills, computer skills, qualifications, family relations, eligibility for veteran’s benefits, work experience, social relations, honors, overseas experience, research experience

04. How Personal Information is Destroyed

Personal information saved in the form of an electronic file shall be destroyed using a technical method that disables the playback of the record.
Personal information printed on paper shall be pulverized with a pulverizer or destroyed through incineration.

Disclosed date: Jan. 1, 2012
Enforced date: Jan. 1, 2012

Guide to the Provision of Personal Information to a Third Party

2. Provision of Personal Information to a Third Party

The company shall only use personal information for the purpose of its collection and shall not use, provide, or disclose it to a third party for any other purposes. However, the exceptions to this policy are listed below:

- When it has obtained separate consent from the source of data
- As specified otherwise by different legal regulations
- Where it is deemed manifestly necessary for the protection of life, bodily or property interests of the data subject or third party from imminent danger where the data subject or his or her legal representative is not in a position to express intention, or prior consent cannot be obtained owing to unknown addresses, etc.
- When necessary for the purpose of accumulating statistical data or academic research, etc., and the personal information is to be provided in a personally unidentifiable form.

Currently, the company provides the personal information as below:

- Recipients of personal information: Engineering Guarantee Insurance, LIG Insurance, Seoul Guarantee Insurance
- As specified otherwise by different legal regulations
- Provided personal information items: Name, resident registration number
- Purpose of use of personal information: For subscription to group injury insurance or identity assurance guarantee insurance for individuals
- Personal information retention and use period: To be destroyed immediately after use
